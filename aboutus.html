About us



- Aria and Mark Lennon in 2007, Orange County, CA.

Orange Records was founded in 2007 by Aria and Mark Lennon in 2007, Orange County, California. Aria and Mark met each other when they both attended Southern California University. The two of them studied Music Business and met for the first time when they joined SCU's radio club. They shared the same aspiration of working as an entrepreneur in the recording industry and to create a unique record label that they envisioned together. After spending four years together in college, they created a business plan and gathered the funds and investors who truly believed in Aria and Mark's innovative record label. Aria and Mark signed up artists they believed in bringing the success to the record label, regardless of their age, gender, ethnicity etc. 15 years later, Orange Records have climbed to the top as being the number one Indie record label that represents the West Coast.
